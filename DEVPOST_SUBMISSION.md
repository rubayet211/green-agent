# GreenAgent

## Tagline

Help freelancers and remote workers earn more by optimizing focus while reducing digital waste and carbon impact.

## Inspiration

Freelancers in emerging markets often work long hours, but poor digital habits quietly reduce their effective income. Too many tabs, context switching, and screen fatigue do not just waste time - they create hidden financial and environmental costs.

## What it does

GreenAgent analyzes a user's work session and calculates a Focus Score and Hidden Cost Score using their hourly rate and estimated billable percentage. It then generates personalized recommendations with estimated time and financial benefit. Users can log chosen actions as Sustainable Work Milestones on Hedera.

## How we built it

Built with Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind, Gemini multi-agent orchestration, Firestore, and Hedera Consensus Service.

## Hedera usage

GreenAgent logs selected productivity/sustainability actions as verifiable Sustainable Work Milestones on Hedera Consensus Service.

## What makes it unique

It connects digital work habits to lost earning potential and sustainability impact in one workflow.

## Demo Flow

1. Select a freelancer work preset.
2. Add screen hours, tab count, tasks, hourly rate, billable percentage, and currency.
3. Review Focus Score, Hidden Cost Score, trend history, estimated lost revenue, and time lost.
4. Pick a recommendation with estimated time and financial benefit.
5. Log it as a Sustainable Work Milestone on Hedera or show honest simulation mode.
